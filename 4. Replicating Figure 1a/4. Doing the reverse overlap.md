## 4. Doing the reverse overlap

```r
YAP_overlap_TAZ_peaks<- subsetByOverlaps(YAP_peaks, TAZ_peaks)
length(YAP_overlap_TAZ_peaks)
```

This answers a *different* question:

> “Which YAP peaks overlap at least one TAZ peak?”
> 

Important point:

- These two numbers **do not have to be equal**
- One broad YAP peak could overlap multiple TAZ peaks
- Direction matters

Beginners often assume symmetry. Genomics does not reward that assumption.
