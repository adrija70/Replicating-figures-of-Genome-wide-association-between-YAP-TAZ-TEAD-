## Finding overlaps between peaks

```r
TAZ_overlap_YAP_peaks<- subsetByOverlaps(TAZ_peaks, YAP_peaks)
length(TAZ_overlap_YAP_peaks)
```

### Tool: `subsetByOverlaps()`

From **GenomicRanges**.

What it does:

- Takes two sets of genomic intervals
- Keeps elements from the **first argument**
- That overlap **any element** in the second argument

> “Which TAZ peaks touch YAP peaks anywhere?”
> 

---

### `length()`

For a `GRanges` object:

- `length()` = number of genomic intervals

So:

```r
length(TAZ_overlap_YAP_peaks)
```

= number of **TAZ peaks overlapping YAP**
